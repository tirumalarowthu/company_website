Join us today to work with the world’s leading technology companies

P2F is one of the fastest growing semiconductor design services companies in the world. As a result, we have now become the go to team for chips being designed at cutting edge technology nodes all the way down to 5nm. As a P2F employee, you will get the opportunity to work with the world’s absolute leading technology companies building innovative products and solutions for a multitude of market segments and industries. At P2F, everyone is hands-on and expected to learn and grow as they contribute towards developing the next generation products that include:

  • Deep-submicron ASICs with gate counts in the multi-millions
  • Multi-core SoCs incorporating CPUs, DSPs, and GPUs
  • Advanced digital and analog design and synthesis EDA tools
  • Sophisticated methodologies for verification, ATPG, and DFT
  • Custom solutions using Xilinx, Altera, Lattice and Microchip FPGAs

We are a highly ethical company with real values and we expect our employees to adhere to them. We offer competitive pay along with an outstanding and flexible benefits package.


P2F offers challenging career opportunities in digital design and verification, embedded software development, analog/mixed-signal RF design and verification, and physical design and layout. Come, design your future with us - take a look at our current openings and apply right away.
